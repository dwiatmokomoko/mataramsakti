## 💡 PRO TIPS

### Tip #1: Start with Google Search Console
This is the foundation. Without it, Google won't know your website exists.

### Tip #2: Google My Business is Gold
For local businesses, GMB is the fastest way to rank #1 in local pack.

### Tip #3: Reviews = Trust = Rankings
Get as many 5-star reviews as possible. They boost rankings and conversions.

### Tip #4: Content is King
Create valuable content that answers user questions. Google loves it.

### Tip #5: Be Patient
SEO takes 3-6 months to see significant results. Don't give up!

### Tip #6: Track Everything
Use analytics to see what works. Double down on what works.

### Tip #7: Mobile First
Most searches are on mobile. Make sure your site is mobile-friendly.

### Tip #8: Speed Matters
Fast websites rank better. Optimize images, use caching, minimize code.

### Tip #9: Local Focus
It's easier to rank #1 locally than nationally. Focus on "yamaha jogja" not just "yamaha".

### Tip #10: Consistency Wins
Post daily on social media. Publish weekly blog posts. Build backlinks monthly.
