## 🎨 CONTENT CREATION TIPS

### Photography
- Use good lighting
- Clean background
- Multiple angles
- Show details
- Include people (when possible)

### Videography
- Stable footage (use gimbal/tripod)
- Good audio quality
- B-roll footage
- Smooth transitions
- Add music

### Copywriting
- Hook in first line
- Clear and concise
- Include call-to-action
- Use emojis (not too much)
- Ask questions (engagement)

### Design
- Consistent branding
- Use brand colors
- Readable fonts
- High-quality images
- Mobile-optimized
